About Me

I have been working as a software engineer at Nike since I graduated from Oregon State University with a B.S. in Computer Science in 2021. At Nike, I have been leading a cross team effort to move internal platform UI monoliths to a microfrontend architecture where I have presented architectural designs and driven cross team collaboration. In my free time, I love playing around with financial data. I built a stock alerts system that collects live data from IEX Cloud and TD Ameritrade that sends me text messages when certain stock prices change. I originally built the system on Kubernetes, and migrated to Google Cloud Functions with Python to scale the system to 6 million daily events while reducing costs. I also enjoy playing soccer, trying out new recipes and traveling!
